# Scout

Scouts are swift adventurers skilled in tracking and reconnaissance. They range far ahead of armies, gathering vital intelligence and striking with speed. 

## Favored Attributes
- Endurance
- Strength

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Blunt
- Hand To Hand
- Light Armor
- Heavy Armor
- Mercantile
- Long Blade
- Alchemy

## Specialization
- Combat
