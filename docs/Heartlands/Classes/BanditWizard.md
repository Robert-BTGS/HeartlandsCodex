# Bandit Wizard

A rare and dangerous breed, Bandit Wizards blend criminal cunning with untamed magic. Their spells are as unpredictable as their motives. Facing them means contending with both steel and sorcery.

## Favored Attributes
- Intelligence
- Endurance

## Skill Bonuses
- Blunt
- Alteration
- Light Armor
- Illusion
- Sneak
- Unarmored
- Short Blade
- Wand

## Specialization
- Magic
