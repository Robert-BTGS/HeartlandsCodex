# Enforcer

Enforcers serve as muscle for nobles, guilds, or underworld bosses. Their presence ensures debts are paid and threats are silenced. Few dare to cross them.

## Favored Attributes
- Endurance
- Speed

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Marksman
- Light Armor
- Destruction
- Block
- Hand To Hand
- Sneak
- Unarmored

## Specialization
- Stealth
