# Blademaster

Blademasters are legendary warriors whose swordsmanship borders on art. Years of training grant them fluid, devastating strikes that few can withstand. 

## Favored Attributes
- Endurance
- Strength

## Skill Bonuses
- Long Blade
- Short Blade
- Block
- Blunt
- Hand To Hand
- Light Armor
- Speechcraft
- Unarmored

## Specialization
- Combat
