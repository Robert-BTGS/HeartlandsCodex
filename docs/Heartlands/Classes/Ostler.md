# Ostler

Ostlers tend stables and caravans, ensuring horses and wagons are battle-ready. They are unsung heroes of travel and commerce. 

## Favored Attributes
- Endurance
- Speed

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Restoration
- Speechcraft
- Mercantile
- Sneak
- Hand To Hand
- Unarmored
- Long Blade

## Specialization
- Stealth
