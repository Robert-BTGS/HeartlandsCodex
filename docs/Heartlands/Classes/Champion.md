# Champion

Champions are heroes, celebrated for unmatched skill and daring feats. Their exploits are sung in taverns and feared by enemies. In battle, they lead by example, inspiring awe.

## Favored Attributes
- Speed
- Strength

## Skill Bonuses
- Blunt
- Block
- Sneak
- Speechcraft
- Unarmored
- Hand To Hand
- Alchemy
- Mercantile

## Specialization
- Combat
