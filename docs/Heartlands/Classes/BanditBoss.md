# Bandit Boss

The Bandit Boss commands loyalty through fear and brute strength. They rally lawless gangs into dangerous, organized threats. 

## Favored Attributes
- Strength
- Willpower

## Skill Bonuses
- Alteration
- Block
- Light Armor
- Mercantile
- Sneak
- Hand To Hand
- Short Blade
- Marksman

## Specialization
