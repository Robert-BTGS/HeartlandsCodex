# Rogue

Clever and resourceful, Rogues survive on cunning rather than brute force. They slip through defenses, exploit weaknesses, and strike at the perfect moment. 

## Favored Attributes
- Personality
- Speed

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Block
- Alchemy
- Speechcraft
- Light Armor
- Herbology
- Illusion
- Mercantile

## Specialization
- Combat
