# Clothier

Clothiers craft fine garments for nobles and practical attire for travelers. They blend artistry with utility, ensuring heroes are as prepared for court as they are for combat. 

## Favored Attributes
- Intelligence
- Speed

## Skill Bonuses
- Blunt
- Block
- Repair
- Sneak
- Speechcraft
- Hand To Hand
- Unarmored
- Mercantile

## Specialization
- Stealth
