# Warrior

Warriors are the backbone of any army, known for strength and resilience. They face danger head-on, wielding heavy weapons and unyielding courage. 

## Favored Attributes
- Endurance
- Strength

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Block
- Blunt
- Hand To Hand
- Heavy Armor
- Unarmored
- Long Blade
- Axe

## Specialization
- Combat
