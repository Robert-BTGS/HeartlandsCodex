# Knight

Knights embody honor, duty, and martial prowess. Sworn to protect their lords and lands. Their oaths bind them as much as their swords.

## Favored Attributes
- Personality
- Strength

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Block
- Blunt
- Long Blade
- Hand To Hand
- Heavy Armor
- Illusion
- Speechcraft

## Specialization
- Combat
