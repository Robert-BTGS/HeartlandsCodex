# Pauper

Paupers eke out a meager existence, surviving on scraps and charity. Their lives are marked by hardship, yet they endure with stubborn hope. Their struggles often reveal the harsh truths of the empire.

## Favored Attributes
- Willpower
- Luck

## Skill Bonuses
- Blunt
- Block
- Hand To Hand
- Mercantile
- Sneak
- Speechcraft
- Alchemy
- Short Blade
