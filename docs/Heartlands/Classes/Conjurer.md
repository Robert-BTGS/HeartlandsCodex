# Conjurer

Conjurers summon creatures and spirits from beyond the mortal plane. Their magic calls forth allies to fight at their side, shifting battles with unnatural reinforcements. 

## Favored Attributes
- Intelligence
- Willpower

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Alteration
- Sneak
- Conjuration
- Speechcraft
- Destruction
- Illusion
- Restoration

## Specialization
- Magic
