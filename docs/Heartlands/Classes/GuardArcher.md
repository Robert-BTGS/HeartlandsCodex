# Guard Archer

Guard Archers protect cities from high walls, raining arrows on invaders. Their discipline and aim make them indispensable during sieges. 

## Favored Attributes
- Speed
- Strength

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Destruction
- Light Armor
- Restoration
- Heavy Armor
- Sneak
- Speechcraft
- MArksman

## Specialization
- Combat
