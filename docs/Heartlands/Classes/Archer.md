# Archer

Masters of the bow, Archers rain arrows from impossible distances with deadly precision. They thrive in open fields and high vantage points, striking before their enemies ever see them. 

## Favored Attributes
- Strength
- Speed

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Restoration
- Light Armor
- Marksman
- Sneak
- Unarmored
- Speechcraft
- Hand To Hand

## Specialization
- Combat
