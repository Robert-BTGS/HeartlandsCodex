# Warlock

Warlocks strike pacts with dark entities, trading service for forbidden power. Their magic is tainted by otherworldly whispers and shadowy bargains. 

## Favored Attributes
- Willpower
- Luck

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Restoration
- Light Armor
- Enchanting
- Alchemy
- Destruction
- Illusion
- Wand

## Specialization
- Magic
