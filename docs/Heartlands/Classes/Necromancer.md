# Necromancer

Necromancers wield forbidden magic, commanding the dead to serve their will. Feared by all, they walk the line between life and death, drawing power from decay. 

## Favored Attributes
- Intelligence
- Willpower

## Skill Bonuses
- Blunt
- Restoration
- Short Blade
- Conjuration
- Alchemy
- Destruction
- Sneak
- Alteration

## Specialization
- Magic
