# Sorcerer

Sorcerers wield innate magic coursing through their blood, unpredictable and wild. Unlike Mages, they rely on raw instinct rather than study, making their power both dangerous and awe-inspiring. 

## Favored Attributes
- Intelligence
- Endurance

## Skill Bonuses
- Alchemy
- Restoration
- Wand
- Conjuration
- Heavy Armor
- Destruction
- Staff
- Alteration

## Specialization
- Magic
