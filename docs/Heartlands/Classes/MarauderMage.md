# Marauder Mage

Blending wild magic with battle fury, Marauder Mages are unpredictable and deadly. Their spells fuel frenzied assaults, leaving devastation in their wake. Few survive their chaotic wrath.

## Favored Attributes
- Intelligence
- Endurance

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Staff
- Light Armor
- Conjuration
- Heavy Armor
- Destruction
- Speechcraft
- Alchemy
