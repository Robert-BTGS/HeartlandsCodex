# Priest

Priests serve the divine, offering healing, guidance, and sanctuary. Their faith shields them against darkness and despair. In war, their prayers can turn fear into courage.

## Favored Attributes
- Willpower
- Personality

## Skill Bonuses
- Blunt
- Alchemy
- Restoration
- Conjuration
- Speechcraft
- Herbology
- Block
- Alteration

## Specialization
- Magic
