# Publican

Publicans run taverns where adventurers share tales and make deals. Their establishments are hubs of gossip and opportunity. 

## Favored Attributes
- Intelligence
- Personality

## Skill Bonuses
- Short Blade
- Block
- Light Armor
- Mercantile
- Sneak
- Speechcraft
- Unarmored
- Blunt

## Specialization
- Stealth
